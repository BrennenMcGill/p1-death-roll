HOW TO PLAY

Are you new to Death Roll? No problem. The game is simple. Here is how it works.

How to Win

To win you must take all your opponent's gold by betting on the outcome of the roll of the dice.

Game Set Up

At the beginning of the game, you and your oppponent each start with 100 gold.

Game Play

The game is played through a series of betting rounds.

  • Placing a Bet

    Each round you place a bet. Your bet must be higher than zero and cannot exceed the amount of gold you currently possess.

  • Rolling the Die

    Once you've placed your bet you roll the dice. You and your opponent each roll a figurative 100-sided die. If you roll higher than your opponent, you win and collect gold from your opponent equal to the amount bet. If you roll lower than your opponent, you lose and your opponent takes your gold. Once gold is transfered the round is over.

  • Next Round

    If you and your opponent still have money you proceed to play another round of betting and rolling. You continue to play round after round until one of you has taken all the gold from the other.

That's it. Pretty simple.
